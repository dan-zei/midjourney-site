Midjourney

An independent research lab that produces an artificial intelligence program under the same name that creates images from textual descriptions

Little backstory

Midjourney was founded by David Holz, co-founder of Leap Motion. It first entered open beta on July 12, 2022. However, on March 14, 2022, the discord server launched with a request to post high-quality photographs to Twitter/Reddit for system's training.
